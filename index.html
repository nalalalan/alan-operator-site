research object

An autonomous system that carries its own proof contract.

Relay turns a vague business goal into a measurable loop: current state, allowed action, forbidden moves, proof deadline, and journaled outcome. The point is not a motivational dashboard. The point is an auditable system where success or failure can be reconstructed later.
